# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: November 2024**

## Introduction

MultinotesAI ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our AI-powered note-taking service.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- **Account Information**: Email address, name, and password when you create an account
- **Profile Information**: Optional profile details you choose to provide
- **Content Data**: Notes, documents, and other content you create or upload
- **Payment Information**: Billing details processed securely through Razorpay

### Information Collected Automatically

- **Usage Data**: Features used, actions taken, time spent
- **Device Information**: Browser type, operating system, device identifiers
- **Log Data**: IP address, access times, pages viewed
- **Cookies**: Session cookies and analytics cookies

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

1. **Provide Services**: Operate and maintain your account
2. **AI Processing**: Process your content through AI models for generation and analysis
3. **Improve Services**: Analyze usage patterns to enhance features
4. **Communication**: Send service updates, security alerts, and support messages
5. **Billing**: Process payments and manage subscriptions
6. **Security**: Detect and prevent fraud or abuse

## AI and Data Processing

### How AI Processes Your Data

- Your notes and content are processed by AI models to provide generation features
- We use multiple AI providers (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google) based on your selections
- AI processing occurs in real-time and is not stored by AI providers beyond the session

### Data Retention for AI

- Content sent to AI models is processed ephemerally
- We do not use your content to train AI models
- You can delete your content at any time

## Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

- **Service Providers**: Cloud hosting, payment processing, analytics
- **AI Providers**: For content processing (subject to their privacy policies)
- **Legal Requirements**: When required by law or legal process
- **Business Transfers**: In connection with mergers or acquisitions

## Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

- **Encryption**: TLS 1.3 for data in transit, AES-256 for data at rest
- **Access Controls**: Role-based access with multi-factor authentication
- **Monitoring**: 24/7 security monitoring and incident response
- **Compliance**: Regular security audits and penetration testing

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access**: Request a copy of your data
- **Correction**: Update inaccurate information
- **Deletion**: Delete your account and data
- **Export**: Download your content in standard formats
- **Opt-out**: Disable non-essential data collection

### Exercising Your Rights

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@multinotesai.com or use the account settings.

## Data Retention

- **Active Accounts**: Data retained while account is active
- **Deleted Content**: Permanently removed within 30 days
- **Closed Accounts**: Data deleted within 90 days
- **Legal Holds**: May retain data longer if required by law

## Cookies

We use cookies for:

- **Essential**: Session management and security
- **Functional**: Preferences and settings
- **Analytics**: Usage statistics (can be disabled)

Manage cookie preferences in your browser settings.

## International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside your residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards including:

- Standard Contractual Clauses
- Data Processing Agreements
- Privacy Shield compliance (where applicable)

## Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
